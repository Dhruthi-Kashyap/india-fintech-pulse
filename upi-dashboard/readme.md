INSIGHT 1 — EXPLOSIVE GROWTH
UPI transactions grew 15x from 2019 to 2024.
From 10,556M transactions to 1,60,000M+ in 5 years.
India is now the world's largest real-time payments market.

INSIGHT 2 — COVID ACCELERATED ADOPTION
Even during COVID lockdowns in 2020, UPI grew 69.4%.
Digital payments became essential — not optional.
This shows UPI is recession and pandemic proof.

INSIGHT 3 — MAHARASHTRA LEADS
Maharashtra recorded the highest UPI transaction volume
across all states from 2019-2024.
Followed by Karnataka and Delhi.

INSIGHT 4 — CONSISTENT MONTHLY GROWTH
Every single month from 2019-2024 shows upward trend.
No single month recorded a decline in annual comparison.
Shows structural shift — not seasonal behaviour.

INSIGHT 5 — VALUE GROWING FASTER THAN VOLUME
Transaction value grew faster than volume in recent years.
Indians are now using UPI for bigger purchases — not just
small daily transactions like chai and auto rickshaws.

# 📊 UPI & Digital Payments India — Trend Analysis

**Tools:** Python | Pandas | Matplotlib

## 📌 Project Overview
Analyzed 6 years of UPI transaction data (2019-2024) 
across 10 Indian states to uncover digital payment 
growth trends, COVID impact, and state-wise adoption patterns.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

1. **UPI grew 15x in 5 years** — from 10,556M transactions 
   in 2019 to 1,60,000M+ in 2024

2. **COVID accelerated adoption by 69.4%** — digital payments 
   became essential during lockdowns, not just convenient

3. **Maharashtra leads all states** in total UPI transaction 
   volume across 2019-2024

4. **Value growing faster than volume** — Indians now use UPI 
   for bigger purchases beyond daily small transactions

5. **Every single month shows growth** — structural shift in 
   payment behaviour, not seasonal trend
