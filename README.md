#  UPI & Digital Payments India — Trend Analysis

**Tools:** Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Power BI

##  Project Overview
Analyzed 6 years of UPI transaction data (2019-2024) 
across 10 Indian states to uncover digital payment 
growth trends, COVID impact, and state-wise adoption 
patterns. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard 
for business decision-making.


##  Key Insights
1. **UPI grew 15x in 5 years** — from 10,556M transactions 
   in 2019 to 1,60,000M+ in 2024
2. **COVID accelerated adoption by 69.4%** — digital payments 
   became essential during lockdowns, not just convenient
3. **Maharashtra leads all states** in total UPI transaction 
   volume across 2019-2024
4. **Value growing faster than volume** — Indians now use UPI 
   for bigger purchases beyond daily small transactions
5. **Every single month shows growth** — structural shift in 
   payment behaviour, not seasonal trend

##  Business Recommendations
1. **Target new users in tier-2 cities** — growth potential 
   still untapped beyond metro states
2. **Build merchant onboarding programs** — value growth 
   shows readiness for bigger ticket transactions
3. **Leverage COVID learnings** — digital payment habits 
   formed during crisis are now permanent behaviours
